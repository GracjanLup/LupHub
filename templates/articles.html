The Era of Short Forms of Media - A Neurobiological Perspective

This bachelor's thesis examines the rise of short-form videos on platforms like TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels, with a focus on their impact on the brain and cognitive functions. It delves into the neurobiological role of dopamine in compulsive usage and highlights connections to sleep quality, focus, and social interactions. The findings emphasize the need for greater awareness and further research into the psychological and social effects of this trend.
