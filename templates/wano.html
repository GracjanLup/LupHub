Your evening at Wano

The itinerary blends the relaxed vibe of a cozy lounge with sparks of focused creativity.

  • 18:30 — Lantern Walk-In Arrive to soft lights, switch off the outside noise, and choose a seat that matches your mood—social, reflective, or exploratory.
  • 19:00 — Shared Table Break the ice with conversation cards or jump straight into the tasting board curated by local chefs.
  • 20:00 — Spotlight Session A rotating guest (sometimes you!) guides us through a theme—design, travel, wellness, or storytelling.
  • 21:15 — Creative Flow Music gets louder, ideas get wilder. Use the sketchbooks and analog gadgets from the discovery wall.
  • 22:30 — Nightfall Notes We wrap with gratitude slips and a parting gift to keep the Wano spark glowing till next time.
Tip: Join the surprise finale on the last Tuesday of every month. The finale is always different, so watch your inbox after you register on-site.
